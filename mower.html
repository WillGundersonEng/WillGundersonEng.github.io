1981 Briggs & Stratton Riding Lawn Mower Restoration

Project Type: Mechanical Restoration / Electrical Systems

In this project, I restored a 1981 Briggs & Stratton riding lawn mower from a non-functional state to full operation. I diagnosed and repaired ignition coil, wiring harness, solenoid, and carburetor components. The process required understanding small-engine electrical systems and mechanical drive assemblies.

Key Work:

  • Disassembled and inspected ignition and starter wiring.
  • Replaced and correctly gapped ignition coil.
  • Cleaned and reassembled carburetor system.
  • Welded and sealed transmission housing to prevent leakage.
  • Tested and verified full system function.

Tools Used: Multimeter, torque wrench, wire strippers, SolidWorks (for small parts design)
